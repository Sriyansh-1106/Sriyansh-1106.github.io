A bit about
who I am.

Hey, I'm Sriyansh — a CSE student from Nagpur who got into AI/ML because I genuinely find it fascinating. I don't just study it, I actually build things with it. My biggest project so far is ASVD — an AI that detects scam calls by analysing voice patterns and what's being said. Started it because scam calls are a real problem, especially for older people around me.

I also have a minor in Business & Finance, which changes how I think about tech. Most coders ignore the "why" behind a product — I try not to. That's also why I got into Power BI. Data means nothing if you can't read it clearly and act on it.

Outside college, I've been a Mukhya Shikshak in RSS since 2019 — I train and lead a group of people every week. Honestly, it's taught me more about leadership and staying calm under pressure than any classroom has.

AI Scam Voice Detection (ASVD)
I built this because I saw how many people — including people I know — fall for scam calls. The plugin works in two ways: first it checks if the voice itself is AI-generated (using pitch, spectrograms, signal patterns), then it reads the conversation and flags suspicious words. Covers 5 scam types. Planning to make it work inside your phone's dialer app.
